Welcome!

This website is a demonstration of my coding capability through a set of interactive score-based games. I sincerely hope you enjoy each one, and have fun!

Here I will detail short descriptions of each game. For the first game, Isle Dig, a 3x3 square is created, and you have to pick 8 of them - one of them fails you! This leads to an overall 5x yield upon success, and no penalty upon loss. Next is Wheel of Treasure, a simple wheel-picker game with around 50-50 winning odds - just spin the wheel and (hopefully) earn some cash! Second to last is Rusty Slots - which is exactly how it sounds. A slightly rigged and yet functioning slot machine - the possible yield on this one is extremely high! Finally, our skill based game - Smooth Sailing! Dodge as many rocks as you can while reaching the highest speed possible to win!
